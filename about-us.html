About Us

We serve patients of all ages and with many different dental needs here at Sriram Dental, but they all feel the same thing whenever they walk through our doors: peace of mind.
Dr. Vijjaykanth, Dr. Shanthimathi and our team make dental care for Chennai families easier than ever because we can provide everything they need all under one roof. Using a uniquely one-on-one approach and state-of-the-art technology, we’ll completely change the way you feel about going to the dentist.

Flexible Office Hours

When would be the best time for you to come see us? What works with your schedule? Whether you want an appointment during the week, on the weekend, our flexible hours will ensure that you never have to miss a moment of anything important to come see us. At Sriram Dental, you can come get what your smile needs right when you need it. We’re here to work with your schedule, not the other way around!

State-of-the-Art Centre

Our high-tech office will not only make your visits with us more enjoyable, but more comfortable and effective as well.

All Ages Welcome

A person's inability to walk or climb stairs should not come in way in he/she receiving the best dental care; so we have a fully operational geriatric dental unit in the ground floor, wheel chairs and chair lift, which help people with mobility issues to get their smile perfected.

Gold Standard Sterilization

Dental clinics have been a focus of attention as dental instruments are possible agents for pathogenic transmission from patient to patient, from patient to doctor and vice versa. With the increasing risk of cross infections by Hepatitis B and HIV viruses, the dental facilities are bound to observe the same stringent rules in sterilization of instruments, and disinfection/maintenance of the operatories. We at Sriram Dental follow Autoclave Sterilization which is the Gold Standard along with UV Chamber. We have installed Over-Head water purifier which gives purified water for all clinic means.
