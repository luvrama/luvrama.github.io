Our Advanced Technology & Comfort

Quick. Easy . Relaxing. All words that people don’t typically associate with their dental appointments, but patients at Sriram Dental always enjoy this kind of experience. We are committed to maintaining a truly state-of-the-art facility, so families can get the efficient and comfortable care they deserve. Using the tools and treatments outlined below, we’ll fundamentally change the way you think about going to the dentist. To come enjoy the best that modern-day dentistry has to offer, contact us today for an appointment.

TMJ Therapy

Does it hurt to move your jaw? Are migraines common for you? Do earaches seem to pop up all the time? If you answered “yes” to any of these questions, it could actually be due to your jaw joint, or TMJ. We have the tools and the know-how to help patients get the relief and answers they need so they can enjoy a pain-free life.

Learn More About TMJ Therapy

Sedation Dentistry

If you’re the kind of person who gets nervous when a dental appointment is coming up, then sedation dentistry is for you. Using either “laughing gas” or intravenous sedation, we can help even the most fearful dental patient glide their way through a relaxing appointment.

Learn More About Sedation Dentistry

Loupes

At Sriram Dental, Dr. Vijjaykanth and Dr. Shanthimathi uses CARL ZEISS LOUPES for closer magnification and pinpoint clarity. Loupes in dentistry help to provide enhanced visibility and thus accuracy during treatment. Loupes are used to see the finer details and findings that could be missed in naked eyes.

Laser Dentistry

Sriram Dental clinic uses AMD Picasso - USA Laser. Lasers can emit an extremely concentrate beam of light. Dr. Shanthimathi can use it to quickly and easily perform a range of procedures without the need of scalpel blades or sutures. With Laser, the dental procedures can be painless and bloodless with faster healing. Lasers can eliminate harmful bacteria from below gum line, painlessly remove infected tissue and even provide beautifying gum lifts in just a single appointment. No matter what is used for, it delivers a comfortable experience and reliable results everytime.

Intraoral Cameras

Every dental patient has had the experience of feeling like a third-wheel during their own appointment, with the dentist and assistant looking into their mouth and talking using terminology they don’t understand. Thanks to our intraoral camera, your appointments can now be a fun and interactive experience. This small, camera-tipped wand fits easily into the mouth to capture high-resolution images of the teeth and gums we can instantly display on a nearby monitor. Now, you’re finally able to see your smile from your dentist’s point of view, helping you better understand your dental health and needs in an instant.

Digital X-Rays

Our office uses X-rays to catch and diagnose dental problems before they are even visible to the naked eye, and to make this tool safer and faster for our patients, we’ve gone fully digital. Digital X-rays take seconds to create images, they don’t require toxic chemicals to do so, and better yet, they emit up to 80% LESS radiation compared to traditional dental X-rays; in addition, Sriram Dental uses LEAD APRON and THYROID COLLAR for extra safety and protection of patients from harmful radiation.
