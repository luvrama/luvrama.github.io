Cosmetic Dentist at Sriram Dental Creating New Smiles

A beautiful, confident smile can do absolute wonders for a person’s life in many different aspects, from personal to professional, and we get to see evidence of this every day here at Sriram Dental. Using the cosmetic treatments detailed below, We are able to help patients who feel self-conscious about stains, chips, cracks, or gaps achieve their dream smile in just a few appointments. Whether you have just one flaw or a few, we can help make them disappear. To get started, give us a call today.

Invisalign® Clear Braces

Think metal braces are the only way to fix your crooked teeth? With Invisalign, you can get the straighter smile you’ve always wanted in the most comfortable and discreet way possible. Using a series of nearly invisible trays, Invisalign enables working adults to get the treatment they need without compromising their professional appearance.

Learn More

Porcelain Veneers & Laminates

With Procelain Veneers/Laminates, our cosmetic dentist, Dr. Shanthimathi, can eliminate a wide variety of smile imperfections at the same time, including:

  • Stains
  • Chips
  • Cracks
  • Gaps
  • Misshapen teeth
  • Undersized teeth
  • Slightly crooked teeth

They both consist of placing extremely thin ceramic coverings onto the front side of the teeth. Porcelain veneers are more permanent and durable and are able to deliver a truly gorgeous smile.

Learn More

Metal-Free Restorations

When repairing a tooth that shows prominently in the smile, strength, durability, and beauty are all important factors that must be achieved. To do so, we use 100% ceramic crowns made by the leading manufacturers such as E-Max, Bruxzir, and Lava. These restorations end up looking so lifelike that even our patients have trouble distinguishing them from their natural teeth!

Learn More

Direct Bonding

Simply by applying a color-matched composite resin to damaged/missing enamel, we can easily sculpt a compromised tooth to perfection. This treatment is one of the quickest and most affordable ways to get rid of minor stains, chips, and gaps. All in all, it usually only takes an hour. Plus, it’s completely painless, and you can trust the natural-looking results to last for about many years. Natually occurring gaps between teeth can be closed with Composite direct bonding or with Porcelain Veneers.

Learn More

Teeth Whitening

Everyday factors like coffee, wine, tea, tobacco, and even aging and genetics can negatively affect the color of the smile over time, leaving the teeth looking dull and discolored. If you’re ready to turn up the brightness of your smile, we have professional strength whitening treatments. Whitening uses a powerful bleaching agent and a low-UV light and Lasers to break up stubborn stains in less than an hour.

Learn More

Gum Recontouring

Does your gum line cause your teeth to look too short or stubby? This is a very common issue, and it’s one we can easily take care of with our soft tissue laser. With it, we can quickly and painlessly remove excess gum tissue to reveal beautiful, previously hidden dental structure underneath. Dr.Shanthimathi can then artfully shape the new gum line along the teeth to give the smile the perfect frame. Even better? This can all be done in just one appointment.
